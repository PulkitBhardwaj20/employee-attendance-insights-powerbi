## 🚀 Overview

The Employee Attendance Insights dashboard is designed to analyze workforce attendance patterns and provide visibility into employee presence, remote work trends, and leave behavior.

It helps organizations monitor workforce productivity, identify attendance patterns, and make informed HR decisions.

---

## 💡 Business Problem

Organizations often lack clear visibility into employee attendance and work patterns, making it difficult to manage productivity, workforce planning, and remote work policies effectively.

---

## ✅ Solution

This dashboard provides a centralized view of employee attendance metrics, including presence %, work-from-home %, and sick leave %, along with trend and behavioral analysis across time and weekdays.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Overall employee presence rate is approximately **91%**, indicating strong attendance levels
* Work-from-home (WFH) accounts for around **9–10%** of total workdays
* Sick leave (SL) remains low (~1–2%) but shows occasional spikes
* Certain weekdays show slightly lower attendance trends compared to others
* Attendance patterns fluctuate over time, highlighting potential operational or seasonal effects

---

## 🎯 Key Features

* KPI cards for Presence %, WFH %, and Sick Leave %
* Trend analysis over time (daily/weekly patterns)
* Day-of-week attendance breakdown
* Employee-level attendance tracking
* Interactive filters (month, day of week)

---

## ⚙️ Technical Highlights

* Built calculated measures using DAX for attendance metrics
* Time-series analysis using date-based visuals
* Interactive filtering using slicers
* Clean and intuitive dashboard design for HR decision-making

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI – Data visualization
* Power Query – Data transformation (ETL)
* DAX – Business calculations and KPIs
